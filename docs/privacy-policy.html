Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tacktic Win handles information when you use the Android application.

Information We Collect

Tacktic Win is designed to help users create, edit, and save football team schemes, player profiles, tactical notes, and visual markings. The app may store the following information locally on your device:

  • team scheme names, formations, player positions, tactical notes, and drawings;
  • player profile details that you enter, such as name, number, position, age, strong foot, and notes;
  • app preferences and cached configuration needed for normal app operation.

Camera Permission

Tacktic Win may request access to your device camera for QR code scanning features. Camera access is used only when you choose to use QR-related functionality. The app does not use the camera in the background.

How Information Is Used

Information entered into the app is used to provide the app's core functionality, including saving football schemes, managing player information, displaying tactical layouts, and restoring your saved work.

Data Storage

The football schemes, player data, and notes you create are stored locally on your device unless a future app feature clearly asks you to sync or share that information. Removing the app may delete locally stored app data according to your device settings.

Third-Party Services

Tacktic Win may use Google Firebase services for app configuration and technical operation. Firebase may process technical information such as device identifiers, app instance information, diagnostics, and service request metadata according to Google's privacy practices.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your saved schemes, notes, or player profiles with third parties unless you choose to export, send, or share them through functionality provided by your device or the app.

Children's Privacy

Tacktic Win is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under the age required by applicable law. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can review the request.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures available on Android and through our service providers to protect app data. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
